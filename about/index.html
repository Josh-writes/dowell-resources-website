About Us

From our foundation in 2012, Dowell Resources Co., Limited has grown into a leading exporter of Manganese Metal, Magnesium Ingot, Silicon Metal, and other ferroalloys and metals in China. We are committed to delivering the best products at reasonable prices, with on-time delivery and exceptional customer service.

Integrity and hard work have earned us a strong presence in global markets—over 70% of our business has come from Europe and North America over the past 13 years.

Thanks to long-term, reliable relationships with our suppliers across China—especially Mg suppliers in Shanxi, FeSi suppliers in Ningxia, and Mn and Si metal suppliers in Hunan and Guangxi—as well as cargo stock availability at Huangpu and Tianjin ports, we are confident in fulfilling our contracts on time.

We always welcome inquiries and questions from both new and existing customers, and we will do our utmost to meet your requirements. Your satisfaction is our success.

We look forward to meeting your business needs.
